🔒 Privacy Policy

SnapZia

Last updated: March 2026  ·  WaveForge

Your privacy is important to us. SnapZia is designed to work entirely on your device — we do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.
📱

1. Data We Collect

SnapZia does not send any personal data to external servers. All your screenshots, notes, categories, and folders are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your data.

🖼️

2. Gallery / Media Access

SnapZia requests access to your photo library solely to scan for and display your screenshots within the app. Your photos are never uploaded to any server, never shared with third parties, and never used for any purpose other than displaying them to you inside the app.

🔔

3. Notifications

SnapZia may request permission to send local notifications to alert you when new screenshots are detected. This permission is entirely optional. You can enable or disable notifications at any time from your device settings. No notification data is sent to any server.

🔗

4. Third-Party Services

SnapZia may use third-party tools for crash reporting and performance analytics. These services may collect anonymous, non-identifiable usage data (such as device model, OS version, and crash logs) to help us improve the app. This data is never linked to your identity and is used solely for improving app stability.

🛡️

5. Data Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. There is no account system, no login, and no cloud sync. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all associated data from your device.

👶

6. Children's Privacy

SnapZia is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

✏️

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notification or an update to this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
